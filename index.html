Why am I removed from room?

Removal happens due to IGN mismatch or not meeting the level requirements. Check details before joining.

How to get room id and password?

ID/Pass is shared in 'My Matches' section 15 mins before start time.

How to find my Esports username and ID?

Check your Free Fire profile banner for your IGN and 10-digit Character ID.

How to join an Esports MOBILE Room?

Open FF > Custom Room > Enter ID/Pass and sit in your assigned slot.

Can I team-up with my friends in room?

Teaming in Solo is banned. Duo/Squad allows registered teammates only.

How do multiple entries in a match?

Only one entry per player is allowed to keep competition fair.

How to cancel an entry?

Go to 'My Contests' and click Cancel 30 mins before match start.

How to edit registered IGN and ID?

Edit in Profile Settings before joining any live match.

Why my account is suspended?

Suspensions occur for cheating or toxic behavior. Appeal via help centre.
